Teacher

HIGH-QUALITY LEVELED BOOKS, SKILL-BUILDING RESOURCES, AND MORE… Raz-Plus combines the features of teacher-centric and student-centric resources to deliver affordable, timesaving blended learning instruction for school and home. The product includes a library of leveled books and additional readers available in multiple formats and languages, targeted skill-building activities, and additional instructional supports to deliver an impactful personalized reading experience for each student.

Leveled Books and Readers

Provide a personal library of developmentally appropriate books and passages to each student in printable and eBook formats, accessible on computers and mobile devices. Assessments and Quizzes Ensure reading comprehension and improved reading performance with printable and online assessments and quizzes that promote close reading and critical thinking. Engaging eLearning Environment Make reading practice rewarding and fun with the Kids A-Z eLearning environment that includes built-in incentives that keep students motivated to practice reading each day.

Mobile Access

Give students 24/7 access to developmentally appropriate reading materials available on all mobile devices with the Kids A-Z mobile app. Interactive Annotation Tools and Features Encourage active reading by using each eBook’s note taking, text markup, and journaling tools that promote critical thinking, close reading, and comprehension. Timesaving Student Assignments Easily assign developmentally appropriate eBooks and additional reading resources directly from resource pages on the Raz-Plus website.

Common Core / 21st Century Resources

Teach skills targeted by the Common Core State Standards and other state and international curriculum standards to help students become college and career ready.
